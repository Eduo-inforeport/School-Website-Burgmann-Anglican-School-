Campus Life

School Front Office

Established in 1999

Thomas and Catherine Gribble travelled thousands of kilometres to settle on this same site. They used some of the local stone and mud to build their home in the late 1800s. You can see some of the remains of their farm behind the Valley Campus today. Its first principal was Dr George Browning who has served as principal for 23 years at 2 independent schools.



Forde Assembly Hall

Was also established in 1999(Most BAS buildings were completed in that same year)

This monumental building has been respected by students, many special arranged appearances have been set here. Every assembly, students acknowledge the Ngunnawal country.



Forde Canteen
Was also established in 1999

Many scrumptious delights have been sold here. This teaches students the way of money management and budgeting. Therefore being a luxury educational building as well as containing some excellent cooks!
